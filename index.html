We are a group of experienced teak carpenters since 2007, based in Blora, Central Java, Indonesia.

Our team can produce all kinds of furniture products, including teak deckings sourcing from legal Indonesian teak logs. Our products are ready for exporting and domestic uses.

"Welcome to Java Woodworking Industry, where we're passionate about crafting exceptional, custom-made furniture that tells a story. We combine traditional Indonesian craftsmanship with sustainable, legally-sourced wood to create truly unique pieces for clients worldwide. Whether you're a homeowner, importers, interior designer, or architect, we'd love to collaborate with you to bring your furniture vision to life. Get in touch with us today to explore how our bespoke furniture can elevate your space."
